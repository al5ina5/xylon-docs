# Welcome to Xylon
 
This document serves as the developer-oriented documentation for Xylon, an Ethereum-based protocol that empowers users to store and manage their identity data in a standardized format on the blockchain.

## What is Xylon?

Xylon is a cryptic Ethereum-based protocol, empowering users to store their identity data forever on the blockchain, ensuring complete ownership and eternal access, while fostering true decentralization. Xylon offers a decentralized, immutable, and user-centric approach to identity data management, giving users complete control over their information while ensuring its availability forever.
